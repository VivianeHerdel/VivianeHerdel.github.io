About me

I am a curious and empathetic UX researcher and PhD candidate in Human-Computer Interaction. I use quantitative and qualitative research methods, along with user-centered design principles, to explore and create solutions that enrich lives and empower individuals. Real-world challenges spark my enthusiasm. Explore this site to learn more about who I am, what I love, and gain a comprehensive overview of my background and qualifications.
